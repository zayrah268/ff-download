Performance Tips After Installing FF

Smooth gameplay depends on how well your phone is optimized. If your device is close to minimum requirements, you should reduce graphics, disable unnecessary animations, and keep frame settings stable. Closing background apps can significantly improve performance because it frees RAM, allowing the game to run smoother.

Internet stability also plays a strong role. If your connection is weak, you may face delayed movement response and slow hit registration. For better match stability, use a strong network and avoid switching connections while playing.

Sensitivity settings are another important factor. Many players ignore sensitivity, but it directly affects aim accuracy and reaction speed. Finding the right sensitivity improves headshot accuracy and helps in close range fights where speed matters most.
